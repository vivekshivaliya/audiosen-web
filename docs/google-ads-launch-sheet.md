## Conversion Actions To Create

- Website page view for lead pages
- Optional: import GA4 conversions into Google Ads after events appear

## Policy Notes

- Avoid copy that targets sensitive health identity directly, such as "Are you suffering from hearing loss?"
- Avoid cure claims or guaranteed medical outcomes.
- Use neutral service language: consultation, hearing care, guidance, fitting, repair, support.
